classification & distribution

The Utahraptor lived during the Early Cretaceous period, specifically around 135 to 130 million years ago. It inhabited current-day United States, specifically in the region of Utah. This is known because Fossils of Utahraptor have been found in the Cedar Mountain Formation, Utah. The Cedar Mountain Formation is known for its diverse ecosystem and paleo-findings which included dinosaurs such as ankylosaurs and iguanodonts species. It's important to understand that the distribution of Utahraptor during its time was limited to the areas where its fossils have been discovered. Since Utahraptor's fossil record is incomplete, it is reasonable to believe that there may have been other regions where it lived but have not yet been discovered or studied.
